That is right - Do not hold back - especially in the name of fun and humour.

Fill the gapz... The game that may not be for all the family! Come up with the most outrageous, offensive, and hilarious statements by matching statement cards with your own selection of cards.

Use the CrCast system to add your own custom card decks and even inside jokes into the game. Simply find the code to the deck you want to add into the game and instantly play it!

Fill the Gapz is free, online and multiplayer. Quickly and easily join your friends or families game on any browser and get involved with the fun that is possible!

The Fill the Gapz game and website is developed by Alfie Fitch and is apart of the variant features available on The Fitch Network.
